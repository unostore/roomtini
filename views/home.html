What is Roomtini?
Roomtini enables elegent use of apartment layouts to create affordable living for everyone. We provide smart furntiure arrangments to create additional rooms allowing rent to superbly low. Luxury amentities once only available to high income earners are now available to everyone.
Do you run Credit?
The leasing office will a credit check on everyone producing a combined score to qualify for the apartment. This makes it easy to qualify, as the combined score means not everyone needs good credit.
How much is Utilities?
Many of our selection of our apartments have highly effecient appliances. Utilities costs are split 3 or 4 ways bringing it down to very affordable levels.
How much is parking?
Parking is always free on the streets. If you would like attached garage parking, fees range from $200-$300 / per month.
How long are the lease terms?
We currently offer 12 to 18 month leases.
How does Roomtini make money?
Roomtini charges a nominal monthly fees for our matching and happines services. This helps cover the cost of partition and late payment protections we provide.
Who's name is on the lease?
All individuals living in the apartment will all be on the lease.
What if I want to get out of my lease early?
You would have to find replacements typical of many apartment leases. In limited circumstances we will work with the leasing office to come with a solution that works for everyone.
Who will I be paying?
All members on the lease will be remiting payent to Roomtini who will be paying the apartment on your behalf.
What can fit in the room?
Split rooms will fit a full bed and in some rooms will fit a queen. A deks and dressor may also fit depending on the setup.
Will there be any stoarge?
Yes, additional storage can be rented for $75 a month or you can be creative and raise your bed to provide additional storage right under your bed!
Are pets allowed?
No, happiness is our #1 priority and many of our matches have shown to have a no pet prefrence. If opinions change, we would be happy to reconsider.
Can couples share a partitioned room?
Unfortunatly we can not. However, couples can choose to rent out an entire room in a roomtini home.
Are there any community events?
Yes, roomtini plans on hosting many community events as our community grows!.
